This site is an independent open-source research project. We are committed to being transparent about how this site works and what data, if any, is collected.

Who We Are

Unmanned Systems Tracker is an independent open-source intelligence (OSINT) database tracking the confirmed and claimed use of unmanned systems in the Russian invasion of Ukraine. The site is operated as an independent research project and is not affiliated with any government, military organisation, or commercial entity.

Website: unmannedsystemstracker.com

What Data We Collect

This site does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use it. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other directly identifying information from visitors.

The following data may be collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, referring website, browser type, and approximate geographic region. This is collected anonymously via Google Analytics / Google AdSense.
  • IP addresses — your IP address may be processed briefly by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and CDN (Cloudflare) for security and performance purposes. We do not store or access IP addresses directly.
  • Cookies — see the Cookies section below.
